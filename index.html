About GDG Buea

GDG Buea

The Google Developer Group Buea (GDG Buea) is a techosystem of electronic enthusiasts and technically-minded hobbyists which grooms hackers, entrepreneurs and researchers by creating an anti-authoritarian culture where Google's technologies are employed to solve the technical problems.

WTM Buea

Women Techmaker Buea (WTM Buea) creates a diverse community in which women can connect, be inspired and encourage each other to realize their passions. They are continually contributing to open source and empowering women around the Buea area to tell their awesome stories.
